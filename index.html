About Us

NavGurukul is dedicated to providing high-quality education to students. Our Himachal campus focuses on a self-study based BCA program, ensuring that students develop the necessary skills for their future careers.NavGurukul is a non-profit organization based in India that offers residential courses in software engineering to underserved and economically disadvantaged youth. Its mission is to provide high-quality education and job opportunities in the tech industry to those who may not have access to such resources otherwise. Key Aspects of NavGurukul: 1. Residential Program: Students live on campus during the course, which helps them focus entirely on their education without the distractions of daily life and economic pressures. 2. Free Education: The programs are offered at no cost to the students, making it accessible to individuals from economically challenged backgrounds. 3. Curriculum: The curriculum focuses on software engineering and coding skills. It includes practical training and projects to ensure students gain hands-on experience. 4. Job Placement: NavGurukul assists students in finding job placements after completing the course. The aim is to secure employment for graduates in the tech industry, helping them achieve financial independence. 5. Community and Support: The organization fosters a supportive community where students can learn from each other and grow together. Mentorship and guidance are provided throughout the program. 6. Eligibility: Typically, the target demographic includes youth who have completed their schooling but do not have the means to pursue higher education due to financial constraints. NavGurukul's approach is unique in that it not only focuses on providing technical education but also emphasizes personal development and community building, which are crucial for long-term success.

Our Partners

We are proud to collaborate with:

  • Eternal University
    • Eternal University, located in Baru Sahib, Himachal Pradesh, is a private university established by the Kalgidhar Society. Recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC), it offers a range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across disciplines like engineering, agriculture, sciences, management, and arts. The university emphasizes a holistic education approach, integrating academic excellence with ethical and moral values inspired by Sikh teachings. With modern facilities, a dedicated faculty, and a focus on research and community service.
  • SBA
    • Shri Badrika Ashram is a spiritual and cultural center located in the Himalayas, established by the revered spiritual teacher Swami Visharadananda Ji. The ashram aims to provide a serene environment for spiritual growth, meditation, and self-discovery. It hosts various programs and retreats focused on yoga, meditation, and teachings of Sanatan Dharma. The ashram also engages in social service activities, promoting education, healthcare, and environmental conservation. It serves as a retreat for those seeking spiritual enrichment and a deeper understanding of life's purpose.
